Classification of Network Attacks using BoT IoT Dataset

It is important to secure the IoT devices from security attacks and ensure the stability of IoT systems, preventing potential security breaches that can lead to privacy violations, data loss, or service disruption. A novel, more reliable detection approach may be offered by machine learning models. Without ongoing human oversight, ML models can recognize attacks and take appropriate action. To solve this problem, a broad range of Machine Learning Algorithms are researched, and the best one is selected to be XGBoost with highest accuracy. Our aim is to illustrate the commonly used protocols in Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) and the associated risks. Utilized machine learning techniques to identify and differentiate between normal and malicious network traffic generated by IoT botnets. Achieved highest accuracy of 99.98 using XGBoost Algorithm

Message Communication and Path Establishment in VANET's using DABFS Protocol

In this project, I delved into the exciting realm of Vehicular Ad Hoc Networks (VANETs), a technology that enhances transportation safety through intelligent vehicle communication. By enabling vehicles to communicate with each other and with roadside infrastructure, VANETs contribute to the creation of safer, more efficient roadways. DABFS routing protocol is studied and used for efficient warning message transmission in bidirectional traffic in this project. By developing efficient communication protocols and systems, we take a step closer to creating a safer and smarter future for vehicular traffic, reducing accidents, improving traffic flow, and ultimately enhancing the overall quality of urban mobility.
